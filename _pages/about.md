I am a passionate engineer specializing in analog IC design. With over +4 years of expertise in the field of CMOS analog & digital circuit design and implantable and wearable medical devices. My areas of expertise include power management, data converters, and low-power IC design. Alongside my technical skills, I possess strong capabilities in research, project management, and innovative problem-solving techniques.

I hold a Ph.D. at the Swiss Institute of Technology Lausanne (EPFL), Lausanne, Switzerland, as an Analog and Mixed-Signal IC designer. I embarked on this academic journey in 2020 with the aim of advancing my expertise in this specialized field. Prior to that, in 2019, I successfully completed my bachelor's degree in electrical engineering with a focus on electronic circuits and systems in Sharif University of Technology, Tehran, Iran.
